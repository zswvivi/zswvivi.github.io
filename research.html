Research

My passion lies in conducting in-depth research and driving progress in the field of AI technology to help individuals worldwide. To achieve this goal, I am working towards developing affordable solutions and services that cater to the needs of those who require them the most. Additionally, I am dedicated to designing algorithms that place a high priority on safety and reliability, as well as creating explainable and interpretable AI solutions.

My current research interest includs:

  • Multi-task generative model— By training a single generative model that can handle multiple related tasks, we can achieve greater efficiency compared to training separate models for each task, especially when the tasks are interconnected. Additionally, this approach enables the transfer or sharing of in-domain knowledge across tasks, which further enhances efficiency.
  • Controllable text generation— To enhance the relevance and usefulness of the generated text, one can design decoding methods or specify particular constraints that ensure the generated text meets the task requirements.
  • Hard negative mining— To enhance the accuracy of models by concentrating on complex examples that the model struggles to classify correctly. Through the process of re-sampling these examples and integrating them into the training data, the model can effectively differentiate between positive and negative samples and boost its overall performance.
  • Explainable AI— To make AI systems more transparent, interpretable, and trustworthy so that humans can better understand the reasoning behind their decisions and use the system more effectively.
